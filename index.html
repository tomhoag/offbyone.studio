01 — The Studio

A studio of one, focused on the details.

Off By One Studio is an independent game development studio. We make games that take a single, distinctive mechanic and follow it as far as it will go.

Every project is built deliberately — design, code, and craft handled in-house, with the patience that small games deserve and the polish that makes them worth playing.
